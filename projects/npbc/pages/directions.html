Directions

Northolt Park Baptist Church is situated off Eastcote Lane, 10 minutes walk from Northolt Station.

From the A40, take the Harrow exit off the Target (McDonald's) Roundabout. Follow Mandeville Road, passing a parade of shops, a BP petrol station, a Post Office and Northolt Station.

Our address is:
Northolt Park Baptist Church, Eastcote Lane, Northolt, Middlesex, UB5 4HN.
